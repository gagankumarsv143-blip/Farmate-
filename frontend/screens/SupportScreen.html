Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a vehicle?

You can book a vehicle by selecting it from the home screen, choosing your preferred time and duration, and confirming the booking. Payment can be made through UPI, cash, or wallet.

What if the vehicle doesn't arrive on time?

If a vehicle doesn't arrive within the expected time, please contact our support team immediately. We'll help resolve the issue and may offer compensation if needed.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellations after this time may incur charges.

How are prices calculated?

Prices are calculated based on the type of vehicle, duration of use (hourly/daily/acre), and any additional services requested. Fuel costs may be included or charged separately.

How do I rate my driver?

After completing a booking, you can rate your driver on the booking details page. Tap on the star rating and provide feedback about your experience.
